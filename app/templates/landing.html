SmartFinance is an AI-powered budgeting app that combines expense tracking, budget planning, receipt scanning, voice input, and an AI financial expert chat. It is fully web-based and completely free to use.

Yes, SmartFinance is a great Mint alternative. Since Mint shut down, many users have switched to SmartFinance to manage their spending, set budgets, and use AI features to get personalized recommendations and answers about their money.

How much does SmartFinance cost?

SmartFinance is completely free! You get full access to all features including transaction tracking, smart budgets, AI voice input, receipt scanning, and bank statement parsing without any hidden charges or subscription plans.

Does SmartFinance work on all devices?

Yes, SmartFinance is a fully web-based application that works on any modern browser — desktop, laptop, tablet, or mobile browser. No app download required. Just sign up and start managing your finances instantly.
